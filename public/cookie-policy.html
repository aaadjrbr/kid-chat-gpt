What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve the performance and functionality of our platform. Below are the services we use that may store cookies on your device:

  • YouTube: We embed YouTube videos, which may store cookies to track views and improve video suggestions.
  • Firebase: Used for authentication, database, and storage services that may store cookies to remember your login and preferences.
  • OpenAI: Our AI-based features use OpenAI's services, which may store cookies for performance and analytics.
  • Stripe: We use Stripe for payment processing, which may store cookies to manage your payment sessions securely.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track and analyze visitor data, which helps us improve the user experience. These cookies collect anonymized data like page views, session duration, and interactions.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. This may affect your experience on our platform.
